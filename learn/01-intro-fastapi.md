# Lesson 1: Introduction to FastAPI
> Date: 2026-05-19

Python is one of the most popular languages, but it has a reputation for being slow. FastAPI is changing that. It combines Python's simplicity with performance that rivals Node.js and Go.

---

## 👨‍💻 Who created it?
Sebastián Ramírez created FastAPI with a clear vision: high performance without sacrificing ease of use. It's so new that Sebastián once couldn't apply for a job that required 4+ years of FastAPI experience—because he hadn't created it that long ago!

## 🏢 Who uses it?
Big players like **Microsoft, Uber, and Netflix** trust FastAPI for critical projects in health, AI, and global logistics. It's not just a trend; it's a robust tool for production.

## 🚀 Why is it different?
- **Speed**: Comparable to Go and Node.js.
- **Community**: Active and growing, ensuring fast bug fixes and new features.
- **Documentation**: Some of the best in the industry—clear, interactive, and even fun.
- **Ecosystem**: It's not just a library; it's a full toolkit for modern backend development.

---

## ✅ Summary / Takeaways
FastAPI is the go-to framework if you want to build fast, scalable APIs in Python. It's built for the modern market and backed by a massive community.
